Healthy Huevos Rancheros

A Quick, Easy, and Healthy Take on a Favorite of Rural Mexican Cuisine

Throughout Mexico's countryside, you can bet that many families start their day by eating some good ole' huevos rancheros. The dish's enduring status as a staple of rustic cuisine is thanks in large part due to its nutritiousness, deliciousness, and simplicity. What if I told you that there was a way to make this dish simpler, more nutritious, and, well, just as delicious?

Okay, maybe I lied when I said I would make this dish taste just as delicious as traditional huevos rancheros; that is because, for the sake of time and health, I have chosen to remove some of the more superfluous (yet delicious) ingredients usually found in this dish and make some key substitutions to get the most "bang for your buck". If you were to make regular huevos rancheros, you would almost certainly include ingredients such as avocado, refried beans, and shredded cheese. While, yes, these items are quite delectable, they contain high amounts of fat, meaning that they are more calorically-dense than their protein and carbohydrate compatriots.
